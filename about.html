Organization History & Vision

Foundation

Our organization was founded with a small but powerful vision—to protect nature and create a greener future. At the beginning, only 10 dedicated volunteers came together and planted 100 saplings in nearby open areas.

Expansion

With growing support and positive impact, our activities gradually expanded beyond a single location. We started working in schools, roadsides, parks, and village areas, encouraging students and local residents to participate in tree plantation programs.

Community Impact

Community involvement has always been at the heart of our organization. We actively engaged students, teachers, local people, and volunteers in plantation drives, workshops, and awareness campaigns.

Sustainability

We focus on planting trees and making sure they grow strong. Our team waters, protects, and cares for each sapling, while teaching the community how to preserve nature. Our goal is to create a long-lasting green environment for future generations.
