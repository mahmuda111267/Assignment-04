Our Mission

Tree plant

A tree is a tall plant that can live a very long time. It has a single stem or trunk and branches that support leaves.

Oxygen supply

Plants keep the air clean by releasing oxygen and absorbing harmful carbon dioxide.

Green Bangladesh

Economic Benefits

Tree plantation offers economic benefits by Providing fruit, wood, and fuel, which helps the local economy.

Why Plant Trees?

Environmental Benefits

Trees filter dust, smoke, and harmful gases (like nitro gen oxides, ozone) making air healthier and produce clean oxygen. Roots hold soil, preventing erosion, while canopies slow rain,

Human Health & Well-being

Spending time near trees reduces stress, anxiety, and depression while improving mood and focus. Cleaner air and cooler temperatures contribute to better res piratory and overall physical health.

Urban & Community Benefits

Shade from trees significantly lowers temperatures in cities, combating the "urban heat island" effect and re ducing energy needs. Trees absorb and deflect noise, creating quieter environments.
